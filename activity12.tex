\item Let $I$ be an open interval and assume $p,q,f$ are continuous functions on $I$ where $f\neq 0$.  Suppose $y_{p_1}$ and $y_{p_2}$ are distinct particular solutions to $y^{\,\prime\prime}+py^{\,\prime}+qy=f$ on $I$.  Which of the following would be a solution to the complementary equation $y^{\,\prime\prime}+py^{\,\prime}+qy=0$? 

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
\item $y_{p_1}+y_{p_2}$  
\item $y_{p_1}-y_{p_2}$ % ***
\item $2y_{p_1}+y_{p_2}$
\item $y_{p_1}-2y_{p_2}$
\item $2y_{p_1}-y_{p_2}$ 
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
\item $-1$  
\item $0$
\item $1$  % ***
\item $1-y_2$ % ***

\item $y_1+y_2-1$  
\item $1-y_1-y_2$ % ***

\end{enumerate}
